## ğŸ“Š **Understanding Your Results**

### **Stress Level Indicators**
- **Low (1-3):** Relaxed, calm state
- **Moderate (4-6):** Normal daily stress
- **High (7-8):** Elevated stress response
- **Critical (9-10):** Immediate intervention recommended

### **Health Metrics Explained**
- **Heart Rate:** Beats per minute (measured during rest and activity)
- **HRV (Heart Rate Variability):** Higher is better (indicates calmness)
- **SCL (Skin Conductance):** Electrical skin response (stress indicator)
- **SpO2:** Blood oxygen saturation (healthy: 95-100%)
- **Sleep Quality:** Duration and continuity of sleep
